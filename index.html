Our Story

More Than a Church.
A Family.

Founded in 2018, Realm Of Glory Surulere is a thriving congregation of people — united by one faith, one Lord, one calling.

Our Vision

We are A People of Praise, Prayer and Sacrifice — and we exist to honor God and to make Disciples.

Our Mission

We believe in the transformation and discipleship of every believer through establishing strong biblical foundations — "Transformed Life, Transformed Society".

What We Stand For

Our 5 Core Values

01
✝️

Christ-Centredness

Jesus is the foundation of everything we do. We exist to exalt His name and make Him known in all the earth.

02
📖

Biblical Truth

The Word of God is our final authority. We are committed to teaching and living the Scripture in all its fullness.

03
🙏

Fervent Prayer

Prayer is the lifeblood of our church. We are a house of prayer for all nations, interceding without ceasing.

04
❤️

Radical Love

We love God with all our heart and love our neighbours as ourselves — without condition or reservation.

05
🌍

Kingdom Impact

We exist to transform communities, influence culture, and extend God's kingdom across every sphere of society.

What We Do

Fun + Faith = 🔥

Every Sunday is packed with activities that make kids excited to come back!

Bible Stories

Exciting, interactive lessons that bring God's Word to life in ways kids understand and remember.

Praise & Worship

Energetic, joyful worship sessions where kids learn to express their love for God through song.

Arts & Crafts

Creative projects that reinforce Bible lessons and let kids express their God-given creativity.

Prayer Time

Teaching children to talk to God naturally, building a lifelong habit of prayer from a young age.

Friendship & Fun

Games, group activities and bonding moments that help kids build lasting Christ-centered friendships.

Safe Environment

All our workers are trained, vetted and passionate about child safety and wellbeing always.
