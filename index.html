Our Story

A Place You Can Call Home

For over three decades, Covenant Light Church has been a beacon of hope, healing, and transformation in our community. We believe every person deserves to experience the love of God and the joy of authentic community.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or deepening a lifelong relationship with God, you belong here. Come as you are — you are welcome.

Who We Are

Our Story

Built on faith, community, and the unchanging word of God.

Our Foundation

Vision & Mission

👁️
Our Vision

To be a lighthouse of God's love — transforming nations, communities, and families through the power of the Holy Spirit and the truth of God's Word.

🎯
Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who are equipped, empowered, and sent to impact every sphere of society with the Kingdom of God.

What We Believe

Core Values

01
Scripture Authority

We believe the Bible is the inspired, inerrant Word of God and the final authority for faith and practice.

02
Radical Worship

We pursue genuine, Spirit-led worship that transforms hearts and honors God in spirit and in truth.

03
Community & Belonging

We create spaces where every person feels seen, loved, and celebrated as God's masterpiece.

04
Kingdom Impact

We are called beyond the walls of the church to bring healing, justice, and transformation to our world.

05
Generational Legacy

We invest in the next generation, raising up leaders who will carry the flame of revival forward.
