MINISTRIES

Our Ministries

Be a part of our amazing ministries

At Realm Of Glory Surulere, we are committed to nurturing a vibrant and diverse community through our various ministries. Each ministry is designed to cater to the spiritual, emotional, and social needs of our congregation, fostering growth and unity among our members.

YADAH VOICES

The Yadah Voices Choir is the heart of our worship experience, leading the congregation in uplifting and soul-stirring praise and worship sessions. Comprising talented singers and musicians, the choir plays a pivotal role in creating an atmosphere of reverence and joy during our services.

The KINGS KIDZ

The Kings Kidz is a vibrant and engaging ministry dedicated to nurturing the spiritual growth of children within our church community. Through interactive lessons, creative activities, and meaningful fellowship, we aim to build strong foundations of faith and character in the lives of our young members.
